NYT x MET

Course Work (group), 2019


In this project my partner, Monica Ionescu, and I created a dashboard web application that takes popular tags in NYT articles, relates them to an image within the MET gallery, and allows the user to further explore that image and related articles. This application allos users to connect art with today's media and culture. It enables the user to further explore a specific piece of artwork by giving them direct access to the MET website. This could help the user discover new art and further develop their understanding of art.
